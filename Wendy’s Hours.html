Wendy's Breakfast & Lunch Hours

Wendy’s is a well-loved fast-food favorite, famous for its fresh, never frozen beef and a menu packed with delicious options for burger lovers. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic Baconator or a cool, creamy Frosty, it helps to know when Wendy’s is open. In this guide, we’ll walk you through their typical operating hours — including breakfast times — so you can plan your visit with ease.

What Time Does Wendy’s Open?

Most Wendy’s locations across the U.S. usually open around 6:30 AM. However, exact opening times can vary slightly depending on the individual franchise.

Pro Tip: For the most up-to-date hours, use Wendy’s official store locator. Just enter your address or zip code to find the closest location and its exact opening time.

When Does Wendy’s Close?

Wendy’s closing hours tend to vary more than their opening times. While many restaurants close around 12:00 AM, some locations stay open as late as 2:00 AM — perfect for those late-night cravings. To be sure about your local Wendy’s hours, it’s best to check their store locator.

Wendy’s Breakfast Hours: Kickstart Your Day Right

If you’re someone who likes to start the day early, Wendy’s has your breakfast needs covered. Most locations begin serving breakfast at 6:30 AM and wrap it up by 10:30 AM. That gives you the perfect window to grab a warm breakfast sandwich or sip on their signature coffee before heading out.

Heads up: Make sure to stop by before 10:30 AM to catch the full breakfast menu!

You can also check Wendy’s Menu with Prices.

Note: Hours May Vary

Keep in mind that the hours listed above are general guidelines. Individual Wendy’s locations may follow different schedules, especially during holidays or special events.

Pro Tip: Always watch for signs or announcements at your local Wendy’s to stay informed about any temporary changes to their regular hours.

Indulge in Your Favorites Anytime

With flexible opening hours and breakfast options at many locations, Wendy’s is ready to satisfy your cravings all day long. Whether you’re craving a juicy burger, crispy fries, or a refreshing Frosty, make sure to use the Wendy’s store locator to find the nearest spot and check their hours. Now you’re all set to enjoy your favorite Wendy’s meals!
